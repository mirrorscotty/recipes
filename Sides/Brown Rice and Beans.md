Brown Rice and Beans
====================

Ingredients
-----------
* 1 cup uncooked rice
* 1 can black beans
* 1 medium onion
* 3/4 cup salsa
* 1 tablespoon olive oil

Directions
----------
1. Cook rice according to package directions. (_see note 1_)
2. Coarsely chop onion.
3. Add oil to medium skillet, and sautée onion until it just begins to turn clear.
4. Add beans (drained), rice, and salsa.
5. Stir and cook over medium heat until heated through, about ten minutes. 
6. Serve. 

Notes
-----
1. To cook rice, in general: boil 2 parts water. Add 1 part rice, reduce to simmer. Rice is done when water is absorbed, about 15 minutes.
