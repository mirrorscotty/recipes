Peanut Butter Cookies
=====================

Metadata
--------
Yield: 30 Cookies

Ingredients
-----------
* 1 cup Sugar
* 1 cup Peanut Butter
* 1 Egg
* 1/2 teaspoon Baking Soda
* 1 teaspoon Vanilla

Directions
----------
1. Preheat oven to 350 degrees.
2. Mix all ingredients together.
3. Press each ball down somewhat with a fork.
4. Bake for 11 minutes.
